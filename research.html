Research

My research has primarily been published in leading international journals within the fields of regional studies and agricultural economics, including well-recognized outlets such as the Journal of Transport Geography, Transportation Research Parts A and D, and Forest Policy and Economics. An overview of these articles is detailed in my research statement.

Selected Publications

  • The causal effects of removing hook-turn regulation on road safety. Transportation Research Part A: Policy and Practice, 205, 104860, 2026 (with Chang). †*^⁺

  • The causal effect of monthly pass programs on public transportation and carbon emissions. Transportation Research Part D: Transport and Environment, 146, 104903, 2025 (with Kim, Chang, and Liou). †^⁺

  • Forest diversity and the distribution of farm revenue - Empirical evidence from forest farms in Taiwan. Forest Policy and Economics, 171, 103411, 2025 (with Lee and Chang). ⁺

  • Does COVID-19 affect metro use in Taipei? Journal of Transport Geography, 91, 102954, 2021 (with Chang, Lee, and Yang). †^⁺

Note: † Journals recommended by the NSTC, Division of Regional Studies and Geography; * Journals recommended by the NSTC, Division of Economics. ^ Journals recommended by the NSTC, Division of Management II. ⁺ Journals ranked in the top 10% of their field based on the Journal Impact Factor.
